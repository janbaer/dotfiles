## Format Specifications

### YouTube Thumbnail
- Dimensions: 1280x720
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9
- Key Considerations: Bold text, high contrast, face expressions, scroll-stopping elements

### Instagram Post
- Dimensions: 1080x1080 (Square) or 1080x1350 (Portrait)
- Aspect Ratio: 1:1 or 4:5
- Key Considerations: Clean aesthetics, brand colors, minimal text

### Instagram Story/Reel
- Dimensions: 1080x1920
- Aspect Ratio: 9:16
- Key Considerations: Vertical, quick impact, text in safe zones

### Facebook Ad
- Dimensions: 1200x628 or 1080x1080
- Aspect Ratio: 1.91:1 or 1:1
- Key Considerations: Clear CTA, product focus, compliance

### LinkedIn Post
- Dimensions: 1200x627
- Aspect Ratio: 1.91:1
- Key Considerations: Professional, muted tones, business context

### LinkedIn Banner
- Dimensions: 1584x396
- Key Considerations: Avoid bottom-left (profile photo overlap), text-focused

### Pinterest Pin
- Dimensions: 1000x1500
- Aspect Ratio: 2:3
- Key Considerations: Vertical, aspirational, text overlay

### Product Photo
- Dimensions: Variable (usually square or 4:3)
- Key Considerations: Clean background, detail focus, consistent lighting

### Presentation/Slide
- Dimensions: 1920x1080
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9
- Key Considerations: Clean, minimal text, supporting visual

### Lead Magnet / eBook Cover
- Dimensions: 1080x1400 (portrait)
- Key Considerations: Title prominent, professional, valuable appearance

### Gumroad Product Mockup
- Dimensions: 1200x900 (landscape)
- Key Considerations: Show bundle value, multiple elements, premium feel

## Style Presets

### Premium/Minimal
- Clean backgrounds (solid dark or light)
- Bold, simple typography
- Authentic expressions (not exaggerated)
- High contrast, controlled saturation
- Person + Text dominant
- References: Dan Koe, Ali Abdaal

### High Energy
- Bright, saturated colors
- Exaggerated expressions (shocked, excited)
- Multiple visual elements
- Arrows, circles, graphics
- Text with outlines and shadows
- References: MrBeast, German tech YouTubers

### Tech/Futuristic
- Dark backgrounds with neon accents
- Glass-morphism, 3D elements
- Glowing edges, subtle motion effects
- Minimal but impactful

### Lifestyle/Aesthetic
- Warm, natural lighting
- Soft colors, film-look
- Authentic, candid feel
- Minimal text

### Corporate/Professional
- Clean, structured layout
- Brand colors prominent
- Professional lighting
- Clear hierarchy

## Reference Image Guidelines

### For Face/Identity
- Minimum 1 frontal photo with good lighting
- Ideally additional 3/4 angle
- Current appearance
- As appears in content/videos
- No sunglasses or obstructions

### For Style Matching
- 1-2 example images showing desired style
- Can be own previous work or references from others
- Model extracts color palette, typography style, overall aesthetic

### For Products
- 1-3 product photos
- Different angles if possible
- Good lighting, details visible
- White/clean background preferred

### For Logos
- PNG format (transparent preferred)
- High resolution
- Note: Well-known tool/brand logos may be generated from model knowledge

## Common Edit Commands

After initial generation, suggest these for refinement:

- "Change the expression to more [emotion]"
- "Make the background darker/lighter"
- "Increase/decrease the glow effect"
- "Move [element] to the [position]"
- "Change the text color to [color]"
- "Make it more/less saturated"
- "Add more contrast"
- "Make the lighting warmer/cooler"
